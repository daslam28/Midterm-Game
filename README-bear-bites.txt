Midterm Game: Bear Bites!

The game provides the user an option between a Polar Bear or a Panda.

The Panda collects bamboo and avoids catching fish. The Polar Bear
collects fish and avoids catching bamboo. 

The user earns points for collecting the correct items, and loses a 
life each time they catch the incorrect items. 

The goal is for the bear to catch as many bites as possible until the 
timer runs out, or until the bear loses all of its lives. 

Controls:
    Left Arrow: move bear left
    Right Arrow: move bear right
    Mouse: click buttons / cards
